# 🎰 Lottery Prediction (彩票预测)

![Android](https://img.shields.io/badge/Platform-Android-3DDC84?logo=android&logoColor=white)
![Kotlin](https://img.shields.io/badge/Language-Kotlin-7F52FF?logo=kotlin&logoColor=white)
![Version](https://img.shields.io/badge/Version-1.9-blue)

A smart lottery analysis and prediction tool designed exclusively for major Spanish lotteries (**Euromillones, La Primitiva, and Bonoloto**). It combines deep historical data analysis, AI algorithms, and unique oriental mysticism (I Ching / Qi Men Dun Jia) to provide scientific number selection strategies.

[🇪🇸 Versión en Español](#-características-principales) | [🇨🇳 中文说明](#-核心功能) | [🔒 Privacy Policy / 隐私政策](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md)

---

## 🌟 Key Features

*   📊 **Visual Heatmaps:** Intuitive charts based on complete historical data to track "hot" and "cold" numbers instantly.
*   💰 **Budget Coverage Planner:** Smart algorithm that generates the highest-coverage number matrix based on your specific budget to maximize winning probabilities.
*   🧠 **AI Deep Learning:** Evaluates historical frequencies, omission values, and recent trends to predict high-potential numbers.
*   ☯️ **Oriental Mysticism:** Unique integration of I Ching (易经) and Qi Men Dun Jia (奇门遁甲) algorithms calculating time and space energy for lucky numbers.
*   🔄 **Auto-Sync:** Fetches the latest official lottery results daily.
*   🔒 **Privacy First:** All data, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device. No user registration is required.

---

## 🇨🇳 核心功能

*   📊 **可视化热力图**：基于海量历史数据，直观展示号码的冷热周期。
*   💰 **智能预算覆盖模式**：根据您的购彩预算，自动生成覆盖面最广、性价比最高的组合矩阵。
*   🧠 **AI 深度预测**：分析出号频率、遗漏值与近期走势，智能筛选爆发号码。
*   ☯️ **东方玄学排盘**：独家集成易经与奇门遁甲算法，利用特定时辰能量推演幸运数字。
*   🔄 **数据自动同步**：每天自动抓取最新官方开奖结果。
*   🔒 **安全隐私**：完全纯本地运行，无需联网注册，绝不收集个人隐私。

## 🛠 Tech Stack (技术栈)

*   **Language:** Kotlin
*   **Architecture:** MVVM + Coroutines
*   **Local Database:** Room Database
*   **Background Tasks:** WorkManager
*   **Network:** OkHttp3, Jsoup (for web scraping)
*   **Monetization:** Google AdMob, Google Play Billing
